Support

Uptick Support is a streamlined system designed to always keep customers online and active

Support Procedure

Urgent

Requests should use the Uptick support number: 1300 657 436.
All requests will be responded to either directly or within an hour if a technician was not immediately available.

Other

All other requests should use the inline support system.
We aim to reply to all tickets within 2 hours of receiving the request, with most requests resolved by close of business.

Standard Support

Unlimited Support

  • Your subscription includes a standard support agreement.
  • This entitles you to unlimited support during business hours (8:30AM to 5:30PM AEST), via phone, tickets or emails.
  • This support is available to your direct employees, e.g. office staff and technicians, but not clients or subcontractors.

Additional Training

  • Additional on-site training can be requested free of charge within reasonable limits as part of the product setup, and for any additional modules purchased or requested post setup.

User Requests

  • For requests that require significant developer time, e.g. new features or adjustments to core functionality, we make a best effort to include this as part of your subscription.
