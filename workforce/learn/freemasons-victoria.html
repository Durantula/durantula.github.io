Freemasons

Freemasonry is the oldest existing fraternal organisation in the world, shrouded in tradition. With over 100 properties and 12000 members in Victoria alone, the safety of our members is paramount

AMBULANCE VICTORIA

With the state of each lodge managed individually gathering a picture of our overall compliance was incredibly difficult and accurately allocating funds for repairs near on impossible.

Preventitive maintenance provided one of the biggest headaches, with each lodge being maintained separately we were never able to reap the cost benefits of having a portfolio our size.

Uptick to the rescue.

Having all the assets in one centralized location enabled us to better plan out upcoming scheduled maintenance, allowing contractors to quote across our entire portfolio rather than on a per lodge basis.
