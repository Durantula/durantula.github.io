All positions at BlockchainIL

Business Analyst (with Funds / VCs experience)

tel-aviv, israel

The Position

BlockchainIL is looking for a brilliant Business Analyst to join our fast growing company and our amazing Projects Operations Team. BlockchainIL’s team is driving our rapid growth via leading innovative and cutting edge blockchain based companies on their journey towards the creation of online economies through a token initiation process and pre and post token generation event processes.

As an analyst at BlockchainIL, you will be working closely with highly skilled entrepreneurs and advising them throughout their business model inception and tokenization process. You will participate in our sound portfolio-management strategy by providing the necessary data for our decision-making process. This is the ultimate position to influence and take part in the blockchain revolution!

Responsibilities

  • Conduct market research, trends and insightful analysis within the cryptocurrency space
  • Explore public and private databases to inquire for business related insight analysis
  • Be on top of business news and related publications to educate with news and trends both inbound and outbound stakeholders
  • Participate and formalize debriefing processes regarding previous business decision making and thought processes
  • Liaise with market analysts, rating analysts and network with industry influencers.
  • Compile advisory reports, business reports and newsletters

Requirements

  • Proven working experience as an analyst / information specialist
  • Experience as an analyst at one of the IDF units or in a VC
  • Fluent professional-English speaking capabilities – a must
  • Strong analytical skills, excellent quantitative and qualitative understanding
  • Bachelor degree in finance, industrial engineering, information science or related field
  • Creative and self thinker - a must
